Reachability is a new feature added in iOS 8. Most people, however, find it fairly useless. ReachApp adds the ability to put the last used app into the Reachability view for true split-screen multitasking. It can also do this with the Notification Center. There are some options to configure and a few more features. See below for a full feature list.


Features include:
• Showing the last application in the Reachability view
• Showing the NotificationCenter in the Reachability view
• Landscape left support for Reachability
• Resizable reachability view
• App chooser to select the app shown
• "Unified" status bar
• "Unified" keyboard


Known issues:
• UIWindows (apps) may be placed incorrectly
• UIWindows (apps) may not rotation or resize correctly
• Keyboard doesn't resize


What to send an email about:
• Crashes (include crash and sys log)

What to NOT send an email about:
• Anything else including visual glitches or apps not showing
