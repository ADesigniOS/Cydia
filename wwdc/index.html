This is a tutorial for getting the WWDC 2014 app onto your jailbroken iOS Device (Because apple is a jerk and set the lowest iOS version to 7.1.x (even though it runs fine on 7.x)

For this tutorial you will need:

- Any iOS device with 7.0.x (this will not work on devices that don't (at least) have 7.0.x)

- A Mac or Windows Computer

- iTunes (needs to be compatable with 7.0.x device)

- AppSync Unified (from http://cydia.angelxwind.net)

Step 1: Launch iTunes on your computer and download the WWDC 2.0 app from the App Store.

Step 2: Drag and drop the WWDC app onto your desktop. (This should get you a WWDC.ipa)

Step 3: Rename the WWDC.ipa to WWDC.zip

Step 4: Extract the WWDC.zip

Step 5: Open the Payload folder.

Step 6: Right click the WWDC file and click "Show Package Contents" (For windows it should just be a folder named WWDC.app and you can just open it.)

Step 7: Open Info.plist inside your favorite text editor/plist editor

Step 8: Inside Info.plist there will be a key "Minimum OS Version". Change that to 7.0

Step 9: Save your Info.plist

Step 10: Drag and drop your WWDC.app file into iTunes. (This is the part that I don't know how to do in Windows.)

Step 11: Drag and drop your WWDC application in iTunes on your device!

Step 12: It should be running perfectly!
