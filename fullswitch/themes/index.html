FullSwitch Theming Guide(ish)

Even though the glyphs in FullSwitch are oh so pretty you may want to change them to your liking. This guide will give you an example of what your theme should look like and details on how to create one.

If you want the easy way out and just want to replace PNGs you can download this example theme and place it on your device with the PNGs of your liking.
