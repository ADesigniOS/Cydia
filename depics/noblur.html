"i do r8 8/8 m8" -Nexuist

A tweak that allows you to remove all of the blur elements on iOS 7/8. It also includes individual options for blur removal.

+ Navigation bar blacklist (turn apps on to disable them).

+ Keyboard blacklist (turn apps on to disable them).

If you find any problems with this tweak I suggest you report them to the bug reporter down below so that this release of NoBlur can be the best yet! Thanks for your help!
